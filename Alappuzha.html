Alappuzha

About

A town with canals, backwaters, beaches, and lagoons, Alappuzha was described by Lord Curzon as the "Venice of the East."
Hence, it is known as the "Venetian Capital" of Kerala. In Alappuzha, Malayalam is the most spoken language. It is an important tourist destination in India.The Backwaters of Alappuzha is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Kerala which attracts millions of domestic and international tourists to the district and employs vast number of locals in the private sector. A houseboat cruise in these backwaters can be booked.
